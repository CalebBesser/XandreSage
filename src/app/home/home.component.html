About The Author:





Alexandré Sage Roddy, the author, was born on Friday, February 13th, 1998. Xandré is a full-time student at Southern New Hampshire University. She currently lives in Chico, California. However, she is originally from the small town of Abita Springs, Louisiana.

Xandré mostly writes High Fantasy Adventure novels but is always excited about any genre of adventure!
