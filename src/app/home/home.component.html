About The Author

Xandré Sage is an American writer of fiction books for all ages, but best known for her new adult science fiction, Our Space. Growing up in New Orleans, Xandré Sage always loved reading stories, especially in Audubon Park. She struggled with Dyslexia but was stubborn enough to keep trying, often being caught reading fiction books with a Dragon Tales flashlight at night. Xandré quickly developed a passion for a plethora of genres, including fantasy, science fiction, thriller, and just about anything that could blossom into an adventure. Tolkien, Brandon Sanderson, George R.R. Martin, and J.K. Rowling are writers that inspire Xandré. Engrossed in stories throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, she found a love for worldbuilding and character development and pursued her Creative Writing degree. Xandré Sage has now written fantasy, science fiction, romance, and thrillers. She currently resides in California with her fiancé and three dogs.
